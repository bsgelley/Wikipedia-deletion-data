This archive file contains a dataset of articles created in December 2012. They were downloaded 
soon
 after their creation and are therefore mostly short. The articles in this set were not deleted
(at least 
by the time we checked, a week after creation); for the corresponding articles created at
the same time 
and later deleted, see the alldels.tar.gz archive. The articles are divided into folders
labeled with their 
creation date. Please note that some sub-folders may contain a folder labeled "Deleted"
with a few articles
in it; these articles were deleted after the week-long horizon we set, so are not
included in the deleted 
set (alldels.tar.gz), but we separated them from the kept articles here so as not
to bias the dataset.
